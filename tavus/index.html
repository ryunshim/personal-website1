What is Tavus?

Tavus is a Y Combinator startup that concentrates on employing highly tailored AI outreach to broaden its customer base. Tavus makes customisation affordable and time efficient due to its AI.

Project Goals

The main business needs were:
1. Rebrand user experience and mitigate frustrations when navigating website
2. Establish a better user flow

My Role

The Tavus website was redesigned over the course of a semester under my direction with a team of 6 designers. To ensure team productivity and project completion, I created and tracked a work plan that includes the project scope, dates, and deliverables. By setting up meetings and creating regular project status reports, I encouraged communication between clients and consultants to make sure project progress always matched customer expectations.

Key Takeaways

• As it was my first role as a product manager, and it taught me the value of clearly outlining the product's scope and setting expectations.
• How to manage a group of novice designers even when individuals are hard to reach and lack communication or progress.
• What it means to be a Y Combinator startup, how it impacts decisions, and how to best suit the company's needs.
• How to best appoint tasks based on the strengths and shortcomings of designers.
